How OwnersUp Works

OwnersUP helps you build highly effective habits to succeed in your goals.

Goal Mapping & Monthly Review Meeting

Each person sets their monthly goals, gets feedback and input from facilitator and their team of solopreneurs. As a team, we break down the goals to weekly actionable steps. At the end of the month, we celebrate our achievements and share lessons learned.
